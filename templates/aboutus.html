About SmartDispute.ai

SmartDispute.ai was created from lived experience. I’m Teresa — a mother, a fighter, and someone who had to learn the hard way how broken the system can be when you don't have a lawyer in your corner.

After spending months facing threats, misinformation, and silence from people who were supposed to help, I decided to build what I wish I had: a tool that makes legal advocacy accessible, clear, and powerful — even if you’re just one person standing up to a system.

Our mission is simple:

  • Help people create clear, legally structured documents — without fear or confusion
  • Use AI to level the playing field for those without legal support
  • Give every Canadian the tools to stand up for their rights, without waiting on permission

This platform is for every parent, tenant, worker, and advocate who’s ever been told to “just take it.” Not anymore.

Welcome to SmartDispute.ai — your voice, in legal form.
